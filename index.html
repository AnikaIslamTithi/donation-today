Food for a family

Feeding those in need of food and water, whether there has been a crisis in their country, or a deficiency of basic foods. We don’t just believe it; we stand by it. Our statement is simple; everyone has a right to food and water.

Shelter for a family

This is a place for evacuees which works as a distributer to provide necessary supplies for people staying in their houses in surrounding effected area. The objectives are to maximize the number of flood victims that can be covered or can reach a shelter within a fixed distance and to minimize the total distance of all flood victims to their closest shelters.
